About Me

I enjoy eating tons of great foods, feeling like shit afterwards, and then working my ass off at the gym the next day to burn the calories.

Right now, I'm a senior at Duke University double majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering and Economics with a concentration in Finance and Minoring in Computer Science. I'm passionate about integrating business and technology to improve efficiency. After graduating from Duke in spring 2016, I'll be an investment banking analyst at Greenhill & Co.. I have previously worked as an investment associate intern at Cambridge Associates and as a business analyst intern at Quicken Loans.

But after having a few internships, I shifted my attention towards finance since it's really a combination of a lot of technical and qualitative skills that I find interesting. While I won't be working full-time in software developmnt or web design, I'm hoping to continue working on my own computer science nerd projects in the off chance I have any time outside of work.

This website is one of those projects I want to keep up with. I'm working on building this website from scratch, without the aid of bootstrapping or frameworks. I'm hoping that this will allow me to develop my web design and coding skills, all while creating an interactive interface for my resume.
